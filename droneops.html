DroneOps: Revolutionizing Drone Operations

At Predulive Labs, we are proud to introduce DroneOps, our cutting-edge software designed to streamline and enhance drone operations across industries. With a focus on efficiency, safety, and advanced functionalities, DroneOps is the ultimate solution for managing and optimizing drone fleets.

Key Specification

  • Weight Capacity : 5 Kg
  • Flight Time : 120 minute
  • GPS: Dual GPS
  • Connectivity : 5G Enabled module with WiFi
  • Range: 50 Km

Key Features

Real-Time Monitoring

DroneOps provides real-time monitoring of drone flights, allowing operators to track drone locations, battery levels, and flight status.

Flight Planning

Plan and optimize drone missions with ease using DroneOps' intuitive flight planning tools. Define waypoints, set flight paths, and automate mission parameters for precise and efficient operations.

Data Analytics

Gain valuable insights from drone-collected data with DroneOps' powerful analytics capabilities. Analyze aerial imagery, generate reports, and extract actionable intelligence for informed decision-making.

Safety and Compliance

Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and safety standards with DroneOps' built-in safety features. Receive alerts for no-fly zones, monitor weather conditions, and maintain operational compliance at all times.

Benefits of DroneOps

Increased Efficiency

Automate drone operations, reduce manual efforts, and maximize operational efficiency.


Enhanced Safety

Mitigate risks and ensure safe drone flights with real-time monitoring and safety features.

Data-Driven Insights

Leverage drone data for informed decision-making, actionable insights, and improved outcomes.

Scalable and Customizable

Scale your drone operations seamlessly and tailor DroneOps to meet your specific needs and workflows.
